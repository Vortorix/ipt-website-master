Complete Transparency

Bitcoin's open source and open access ledger means anyone can view transactions, anytime. With Bitcoin, you can provide proof for balances and transactions anytime, without doubt.

Standardised Fees

Bitcoin transactions are borderless and standardised. Transaction fees aren't limited to particular countries, and aren't adjusted based on where you are sending from or to. Transacting larger amounts don't result in larger fees, unlike banks.
